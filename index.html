How to Throw a Fabulous Virtual Awards Party

By Parker Herren, 6 October 2020

Crowded parties, paparazzi swarms, and packed theaters are so 2019. Virtual, health-conscious parties are all the rage this year!

But who says you can't keep yourself and your friends safe in style?

Whether it's a glitzy award show celebration or a premiere screening for this year's Oscar hopeful, a little effort can go a long way toward having a Zoom get together your friends will be talking about for weeks.

Follow these tips and tricks for the hottest virtual party in Hollywood:

Costumes

Dress up! Whether it's a glam gown and full beat or cosplaying as your favorite character/actor, a fun costume sets the mood not only for your guests, but for you!

Background

Transport yourself! Perhaps we can't attend flashy premieres irl, but it's all about imagination! Share your Zoom background with guests in advance for extra immersion!

Snacks

What's a screening without popcorn? A delicious drink? Just like your background, bonus points for coordinating snacks with other guests!

Ballots

Why have a friendly party when you can have cut-throat competition instead? Put money on your picks to really get wild.

Social Media

We're in the digital age and your Zoom party is not confined to its virtual walls! Engage with the world and share the juiciest tweets with your guests.
